      UK parliament members starting to be interested in Vitamin D for COVID-19

      October 8, 2020

      Vitamin D interview on Sky News (08/10/20)

      Interview Oct 8, 2020 Rupa Huq

      Meeting Oct 7, 2020: David Davis, Matt Hancock, Rupa Huq

      Her comments - from email

      • I am delighted to report that the meeting with the Health Secretary last night on Vitamin D was constructive and positive.

      • I am glad he was willing to listen with an open mind to the evidence on the vitamin and its benefits in tackling Covid-19, which is fast becoming difficult to ignore.

      • I spoke with Adam Boulton on Sky News today to discuss the meeting, and why the Government must include Vitamin D as part of its armoury in the UK’s fight against this wretched virus.

      Who is who: by Robin Whittle

      Vitamin-D-ophile British MPs,

      • Rupa Huq, Labour, Remainiac Wikipedia link

      • David Davis, Conservative, chairman of the Brexit committee at one stage, former chairman of the Conservative party and, in the past, a possible leader of the party.

      • Matt Ridley, Conservative in the House of Lords, is also on the case and has been writing articles in the media about Vitamin D and COVID-19 for months, including with David Davis.
