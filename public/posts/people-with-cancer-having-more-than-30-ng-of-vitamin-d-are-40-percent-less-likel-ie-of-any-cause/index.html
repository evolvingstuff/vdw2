!!!!Association between the Serum Vitamin D Concentration and All-Cause and Cancer-Specific Mortality in Individuals with Cancer
Nutrition and Cancer  Nov 2023 https://doi.org/10.1080/01635581.2023.2279233

We aimed to explore the association between the serum 25-hydroxyvitamin D concentration and all-cause and cancer-specific mortality in 2,463 adult patients with cancer from the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey 2007‚Äì2018. We linked mortality data from the survey to the National Death Index records up to December 31, 2019.
