*  **<span style="color:#F00;">600 IU is enough</span>**  

   *  **Nope - [US govt admitted the mistake](/posts/us-admits-math-mistake-was-made-in-2010-in-estimating-vitamin-d-but-will-not-change-recommendations), &nbsp;[19 organizations](/posts/4000-iu-of-vitamin-d-is-ok-19-organizations-agree-2018) and [Dr. Holick](/posts/vitamin-d-at-least-4000-iu-to-achieve-40-60-ng-and-reduce-risk-of-early-death-holick) agree: 4,000 IU is recommended**  

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Everyone should get the same dose</span>**  

   *  **Nope - proper dose varies with [weight/obesity](/posts/obese-need-2x-to-3x-more-vitamin-d), [skin color genes](/posts/response-to-uv-varies-more-with-pigment-genes-and-age-than-skin-color), [disease](/posts/diseases-that-may-be-related-via-low-vitamin-d), etc**  

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">2 months restores levels, on-going dose is not needed</span>**  

   *  **Nope - restoration often needs[4-6 months](/posts/vitamin-d-response-time-is-3-6-months-not-much-benefit-in-first-4-months-rct) (without a [loading dose](/posts/overview-loading-of-vitamin-d),** 

   * &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; **and needs to be continued for life** 

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Daily dosing is best</span>**  

   *  **Nope - [32 trials proved benefit from weekly or bi-weekly](/posts/one-pill-every-two-weeks-gives-you-all-the-vitamin-d-most-adults-need),** 

   * &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; **[perhaps infrequent dosing is better than daily](/posts/optimal-vitamin-d-supplementation-strategies-d3-weekly-or-monthly)** 

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Can get enough Vitamin D from milk</span>**  

   *  **Nope - <a href="/posts/10-glasses-of-whole-milk-to-get-just-800-iu" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This link has an unknown page_id: 8849">10+ glasses of whole milk to get just 800 IU</a>**
