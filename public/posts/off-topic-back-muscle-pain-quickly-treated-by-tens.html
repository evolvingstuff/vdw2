      Off topic: back muscle pain quickly treated by TENS

      January 25, 2023

      A few times a year a tiny portion of my back would seize up making movement and walking painful

      In April 2022 I purchased a $32 TENS machine from Amazon

      The next opportunity to use the TENS machine was in Jan 2023

      Pain is almost entirely gone after ~ four 30-minute treatments in a single day

      It has lots of options - but I only used the defaults on level 3 intensity out of 10
