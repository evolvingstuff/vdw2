#### B-50 and B-100 contents vary:

 **B-50 Complex** 

Thiamin (B1): 50 mg

Riboflavin (B2): 50 mg

Niacin (B3): 50 mg

Vitamin B6: 50 mg

Folate (B9): 400-665 mcg

Vitamin B12: 50-150 mcg

Biotin (B7): 50-600 mcg

Pantothenic Acid (B5): 50 mg

 **B-100 Complex** 

Thiamin (B1): 100 mg

Riboflavin (B2): 100 mg

Niacin (B3): 100 mg

Vitamin B6: 100 mg

Folate (B9): 400-800 mcg

Vitamin B12: 100 mcg

Biotin (B7): 100-1000 mcg

Pantothenic Acid (B5): 100 mg
