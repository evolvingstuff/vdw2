Arch Osteoporos. 2019 Apr 30;14(1):50. [doi: 10.1007/s11657-019-0589-y.](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11657-019-0589-y.)

Weaver CM1, Bischoff-Ferrari HA2,3,4, Shanahan CJ5.

It correctly assumes a 14% reduction in hospital costs due to reduced fractures

It fails to consider the considerable non-hospital cost-savings of fractures

&nbsp; &nbsp; e.g. Rehab, Physical therapy, lost job, nursing homes, in-home care, etc.

It fails to consider the cost-savings of reduced falls (without fractures)

It fails to consider the considerable cost savings of increased Vitamin D resulting in reduced health problems
