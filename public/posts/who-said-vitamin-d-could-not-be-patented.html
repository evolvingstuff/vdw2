      Who said vitamin D could not be patented

      September 23, 2010

      A few of the 1,000+ vitamin D entries for US and International Patents.

      Most were for Vitamin D analogs and vitamin D testing.

      These provide some insight as to what vitamin D might help with, and shows vitamin D uses which cannot be mentioned for fear of having to pay patent royalties.

      Interesting to note that a majority of the patents are for TREATING, not PREVENTING

      A majority of these vitamin D patents are owned by Big Pharma .
