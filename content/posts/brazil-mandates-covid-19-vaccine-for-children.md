#### [Brazil mandates COVID-19 vaccine for children](https://frontline.news/post/brazil-mandates-covid-19-vaccine-for-children?utm_source=substack&utm_medium=email)

Ages 0.5 to 6

"The Lula Government's recommendation will prioritize children from 6 months to under 5 years of age and the groups most at risk of developing severe forms of the disease: the elderly, immunocompromised, pregnant and postpartum women, health workers, people with comorbidities, indigenous people, riverside dwellers and quilombolas, people living in long-term institutions and their workers, people with permanent disabilities, people deprived of liberty over 18 years of age, teenagers and young people complying with socio-educational measures, employees of the deprivation of liberty system and people living on the streets."
