Submitted to the FDA Sept 22, 2021

from page 23

In a clinical study of participants 18 through 55 years of age, the most commonly reported adverse reactions (≥10%) following administration of a booster dose were   
pain at the injection site (83.0%),   
fatigue (63.7%),   
headache (48.4%),   
muscle pain (39.1%),   
chills (29.1%), and   
joint pain (25.3%).

No adverse reaction data on other age groups
