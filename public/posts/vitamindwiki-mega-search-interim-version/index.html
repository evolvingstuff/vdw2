                                        August 7, 2023

                                        This is an interim version of searching for words or phrases in VitaminDWiki

                                          As you type, it shows the number of lines that match

                                        Only highlights the results that match your search, but shows the rest

                                        Right-clicking on an item opens the link in a new tab

                                        • It will find the words only in the same order as entered

                                          • Example: It will find Breast Cancer, but not Cancer Breast

                                        Interim version with a Computer:

                                        1. Enter Ctrl-F

                                        2. Enter word(s) or phrase

                                        Interim version with a Mobile device:

                                        81a) Select More (triple dot in Chrome)

                                        1b) Select Find on page

                                        1. Enter in the word(s) of interest

                                        Mega-search spans VitaminDWiki :

                                        1. Health Problems

                                        2. Menu Items

                                        3. Titles of popular pages

                                        4. Titles of recent pages

                                        5. Most-viewed pages

                                        6. Pages that include the phrases “many studies” or several studies"

                                        You can also search in another language

                                        1. Select Translate in the upper right of the page
