#### [Looking for Links Between Testosterone and Vitamin D Levels](http://www.hcplive.com/medical-news/looking-for-links-between-testosterone-and-vitamin-d-levels)

* presented  at the American Urological Association’s annual meeting in New Orleans 

* ”The men who failed to correct the deficiency, on average, had total testosterone levels of 319.6 ng/L, while men who took supplements, and men with naturally sufficient levels of vitamin D, had total testosterone levels that averaged 341.7 ng/L (P=.012)” 

Article did not mention dose size

Note: as little as 2,000 IU might be enough for most men, but some will need 4,000 IU or more.
