#### U.S. Maternal Mortality Hits Highest Level Since 1965 - Wall Street Journal

 **[WSJ](https://www.wsj.com/articles/u-s-maternal-mortality-hits-highest-level-since-1965-f9829776) - behind paywall &nbsp; &nbsp; <i class="fas fa-file-pdf" style="margin-right: 0.3em;"></i><a href="https://d378j1rmrlek7x.cloudfront.net/attachments/pdf/wsj-maternal-deaths.pdf">Click here for the PDF</a>** 

* "Some pregnancy and postpartum complications probably stem from [rising obesity](https://www.wsj.com/articles/pediatricians-recommend-weight-loss-drugs-for-obese-children-11673213563?mod=article_inline) and declining heart health in the U.S., doctors said. Around 42% of U.S. adults [are considered obese](https://www.cdc.gov/obesity/data/adult.html), nearly half have [high blood pressure](https://www.cdc.gov/bloodpressure/facts.htm#:~:text=Nearly%20half%20of%20adults%20in,are%20taking%20medication%20for%20hypertension.), around 11% [have diabetes](https://www.cdc.gov/diabetes/library/reports/reportcard/national-state-diabetes-trends.html) and 38% [have prediabetes](https://www.cdc.gov/diabetes/data/statistics-report/prevalence-of-prediabetes.html), the CDC said. "

* "Black mothers were the most affected. The mortality rate among Black women was 2.6 times as much as the rate for white women in 2021, and 30% of maternal deaths were among Black women."

* "The mortality rate for Hispanic mothers overtook that among white women in 2021,..."
