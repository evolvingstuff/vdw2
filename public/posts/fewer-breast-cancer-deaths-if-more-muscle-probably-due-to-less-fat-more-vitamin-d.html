      Fewer Breast Cancer deaths if more muscle (probably due to less fat, more Vitamin D)

      April 7, 2018

      FACT: People with high fat content have lower levels of Vitamin D

      • Obese are 3.4 X more likely to be Vitamin D deficient – meta-analysis Sept 2015

      • Perhaps women have lower levels of Vitamin D because vitamin D hides in fat

      FACT: People with low vitamin D at higher risk of dying of Breast Cancer

      • Death due to breast cancer reduced 40 percent if high vitamin D – meta-analysis April 2014

      • Breast Cancer prevented by Vitamin D (most studies concur) – Dec 2017

      FACT: Other diseases are associated with obesity - probably due to lower vitamin D

      • Multiple Sclerosis 40% more likely in obese than in overweight (Mendelian randomization) Jan 2018

      • Obesity linked to 13 cancers , the linkage is probably vitamin D – Aug 2016

      • Autistic child 3X more likely if mother was obese and diabetic (both associated with low vitamin D) - Feb 2015

      • Diabetes 6X more likely for those abdominal obese who have insufficient vitamin D – June 2012
