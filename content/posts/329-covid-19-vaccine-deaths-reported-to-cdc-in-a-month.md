 **The numbers reflect the latest data available as of Jan. 22 from the CDC’s [Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System website](https://vaers.hhs.gov/reportevent.html). Of the 329 reported deaths, 285 were from the U.S., and 44 were from other countries. The average age of those who died was 76.5.** 

 **World-wide (mainly US) reports from Dec. 14, 2020 to Jan. 22** 

 **These are reports – not confirmed** 

 **You can access  [Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System](https://vaers.hhs.gov/reportevent.html)   
 &nbsp; &nbsp; and get reports by: type of vaccine, part of the world, age, time period, type of adverse event, and scores of other parameters**
