      USANA found 5000 IU resulted in 50 ng - Winter 2010

      December 2, 2010

      USANA is a large supplement provider. “Nutritionals you can trust”

      Started in 1992, in 14 countries in 2010, pricey but very nice

      They do not appear to sell 5,000 IU capsules, yet their chart shows that 5,000 is how much many people need

      Their solid tablet has 2000 IU and 30 mg of vitamin K (does not say: K1 vs K2)

      There have been several reports of problems with vitamin D in solid tablets - about 1/3 the benefit as capsule form

      But, the chart below implies that USANA solid tablet is OK

      This assumes that most of the USANA customers were taking USANA supplements, and not just those at the left side of the graph.

      Note the great number of USANA customers taking 2,000 or 4,000 on the graph.

      This correlates with USANA selling 2,000 IU tablets
