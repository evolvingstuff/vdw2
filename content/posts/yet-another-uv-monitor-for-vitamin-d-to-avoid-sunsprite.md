### [Sunsprite](http://www.sunsprite.com/%20) Does not appear to adjust for

1. UVB vs UVA – which varies a LOT during the day

1. Age

1. Amount of clothing

1. Skin color

1. Weight

#### They also recommend staying out of the noonday sun – the very time when you can get the most Vitamin D
