*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Oil-based Vitamin D is OK</span>**  

   *  **Nope - Oil-based D is not OK if no [oil at the meal](/posts/cooked-dried-beans-or-peas-pulses-reduce-uptake-of-vitamin-d-vitamin-k-by-the-gu-several-studies) or no <a href="/posts/gallbladder-removal-and-vitamin-d-deficiency" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This post/category does not exist yet: Gallbladder removal and vitamin D deficiency">gallbladder</a>**  

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Only need Vitamin D</span>**  

   *  **Nope - <a href="/posts/vitamin-d-cofactors-in-a-nutshell" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This post/category does not exist yet: Vitamin D Cofactors in a nutshell">Cofactors</a> are needed if take >4,000 IU  (Magnesium, Vit K, Omega-3, etc)**  

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Can get enough Vitamin D from food</span>**  

   *  **Nope: No longer does meat, lard have much Vitamin D as it used to**  

   *  **Nope: <a href="/posts/food-sources-and-vitamin-d" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This post/category does not exist yet: Food Sources and Vitamin D">No longer do people eat much fatty or wild caught fish</a>**  

   *  **Nope: No longer do people often eat organ meats**  

   *  **Nope: No longer does food have enough <a href="/posts/overview-magnesium-and-vitamin-d" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This post/category does not exist yet: Overview Magnesium and vitamin D">Magnesium</a> to utilize Vitamin D**  

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Very little Vitamin D in meat and eggs</span>**  

   *  **Nope: <a href="/posts/vitamin-d-from-animal-increases-6x-when-consider-that-already-processed-by-animal-livers" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This post/category does not exist yet: Vitamin D from animal increases 6X when consider that already processed by animal livers">Studies rarely consider</a> the semi-activated form Vitamin D**  

   * &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;  **underestimated the amount available from meat and eggs by a factor of 6 to 9**  

   * &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;  **the total Vitamin D (un-activated and semi-activated) is still ~5X less than it had been**  

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">>10,000 IU can be toxic</span>**  

   *  **Nope - <a href="/posts/overview-toxicity-of-vitamin-d" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This post/category does not exist yet: Overview Toxicity of vitamin D">Large doses are OK if cofactors are taken correctly</a>**  

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Blood test: Indicates how much Vitamin D is getting to tissue</span>**  

   *  **Nope - Vit D can be limited by [one or more of 5 genes downstream from Vit D test](/categories/genetics),**  

   * &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; **especially the [Vitamin D Receptor gene](/categories/vitamin-d-receptor)**
