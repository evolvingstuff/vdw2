      Many hints that vitamin D megadoses cure some diseases

      May 6, 2013

      Unaware of any studies of meg-doses of vitamin D

      There have been several indications that getting lots of vitamin D (> 20,000 IU daily) can cure some diseases

      1. Appears that Vitamin D can induce remission in recent Multiple Sclerosis 1,000 patients 20,000 IU to 140,000 IU daily

      2. Dr. Cannell on Autism – Spring 2013 3 moms accidentally gave 60,000 IU daily - rapid recovery from Autism

      3. ovarian cancer had metastasized, give only months to live, internal bleeding 50,000 IU daily for 3 months

      Totally cured, then dropped back to 50,000 IU weekly.

      1. Traumatic Brain Injury reduced 2X by about 20,000 IU of vitamin D daily for 5 days

      with progestrone. Similar to a loading dose

      1. Vitamin D repaired nerve in rat (50,000 IU daily dose equivalent for human) – June 2013 35,000 IU for average weight adult
