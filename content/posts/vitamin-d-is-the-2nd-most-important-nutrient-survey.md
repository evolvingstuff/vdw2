# From [USA Today Aug 2011](http://yourlife.usatoday.com/fitness-food/diet-nutrition/story/2011/08/Functional-foods-provide-more-than-just-nutrition/49791906/1)

## Survey of 1000 people of Functional Foods by Cogent Research

-The nutrients and components considered most important by Americans

1. calcium (92%), 

1.  **<span style="color:#F00;">vitamin D (90%),</span>**  

1. protein (87%), 

1. B vitamins (86%), 

1. omega-3 fatty acids (85%), 

1. probiotics (81%) and 

1. fiber (79%).
