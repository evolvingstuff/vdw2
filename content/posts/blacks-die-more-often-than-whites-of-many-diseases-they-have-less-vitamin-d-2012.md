 **[Cancer Facts & Figures for African Americans](https://www.cancer.org/research/cancer-facts-statistics/cancer-facts-figures-for-african-americans.html) Cancer.org** 

* “African Americans have the highest death rate and shortest survival of any racial and ethnic group in the US for most cancers”

* Has a huge number of tables and charts, Note: Vitamin D is not mentioned

 **<span style="color:#00F;">Leading Causes of Death</span>**   as of March 2018

 **| | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| All Ages Death rate | Black | White | Ratio |
| Heart diseases  | 217  | 171 | <span style="color:#F00;">1.27</span> |
| Cancer  | 199 | 170 | <span style="color:#F00;">1.17</span> |
| Cerebrovascular diseases | 51 | 36 | <span style="color:#F00;">1.4</span> |
| Diabetes  | 40 | 19 | <span style="color:#F00;">2.0</span>  |** 

Rates per 100,000 &nbsp; &nbsp; Age adjusted&nbsp; &nbsp; Non-Hispanic
