Reporting on an unidentified study

* “335 women who attended a fertility clinic in Milan in 2012”

* “.. embryo implantation rate for this group was just 13% compared to 21% for women who have sufficient”

* No mention of what “sufficient level” is –  most likely 20 ng or 30 ng of Vitamin D

* No mention of influence of success due to vitamin D level of sperm donor
