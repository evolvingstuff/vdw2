### [How do primary care physicians and practitioners view and implement vitamin D in their practice?](http://www.vitamindcouncil.org/blog/how-do-primary-care-physicians-and-practitioners-view-and-implement-vitamin-d-in-their-practice/) Vitamin D Council

#### Four themes  found

1. The primary driver of demand is the patient 

1. Reflexive acceptance of patient demand without additional investigation 

1. Incorporation into patient care as a diagnostic tool 

1. Doubt – “A substantial number of participants viewed the patient-driven demand for vitamin D testing and treatment with skepticism.”
