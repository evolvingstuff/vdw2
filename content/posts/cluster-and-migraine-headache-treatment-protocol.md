##### Starting the Anti-Inflammatory Regimen

ItвАЩs important to start this regimen and treatment protocol with the accelerated vitamin D3 loading schedule for faster headache pain-free response times.¬† This is done with a total loading dose 600,000 IU (15 mg) vitamin D3 spread over 12 days at 50,000 IU/day or 6 days at 100,000 IU/day.
