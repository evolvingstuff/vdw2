      Shareable COVID-19 Anti-Panic Posters Justin Hart Nov 21, 2021

      Using information from the iii.org we were able to piece together comparable risks of dying of COVID vs other risks.

      Obese men dominate the mortality tables for COVID-19. Women fare much better.

      This one is more controversial… but looking at the mortality tables anything under 45 is almost unmeasurable. Of late, things have gotten worse for this age group in the all-cause mortality waves - but that has more to do with lockdowns than anything else as I see it.

      Once you get down under 20 the chances of dying of COVID are a 1000x less than an 80 year old.
