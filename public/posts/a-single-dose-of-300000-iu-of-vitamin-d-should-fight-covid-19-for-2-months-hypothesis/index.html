      A single dose of 300,000 IU of vitamin D should fight COVID-19 for 2 months - Hypothesis

      August 21, 2020

      HYPOTHESIS: A Single Large Dose of Vitamin D Could be Used as a Means of Coronavirus Disease 2019 Prevention and Treatment

      Drug Design, Development and Therapy Dovepress

      Guoqiang Liu. Tianpei Hong, Jin Yang yangjin@bjmu.edu.cn

      Dept of Endocrinology and Metabolism, Peking U. Third Hospital, Beijing 100191, People’s Republic of China

          • 36 studies cited this study as of Jan 2023
          • RCTs using Vitamin D to fight COVID-19

      • Appears that a bolus of 300,000 IU of vitamin D is a standard of practice for many health problems in this hospital in China

      • No Vitamin D test is needed before taking the 300,000 IU

      • Paper says that the bolus dose should last for 2 or 3 months

        • but it appears that vitamin D levels would drop too low to fight COVID-19 after 2 months

      • It might be preferable to have a “booster” of 100,000 IU monthly

        • instead of 300,000 every 2 months

      • For prevention, some doctors prefer 50,000 IU daily for a week, followed by 50,000 IU weekly

      • Probably need to increase the initial and maintenance doses be 2X larger for those who areobese

      • Approximately 1 in 300 people will get an adverse reaction (itchy skin) to Vitamin D due to a lack of Magnesium

        • Should consider taking 400 mg of Magnesium concurrently with the Vitamin D

      • To quickly fight a COVID-19 infection a different form of VItamin D should be considered

        • Response of bolus Vitamin D typically start in 12 hours

        • Response to sublingual Nanoemulsion Vitamin D appears to start in 3 hours

          • Note: The response to a Vitamin D injection takes several days

      • Keep Vitamin D on-hand for emergencies 200,000 IU in case of symptoms

      • Emergency treatments if get COVID-19 symptoms - Oct 2021

      • French Consensus – 200,000 IU of Vitamin D if get COVID-19 – Dec 22, 2020

      • COVID-19 patients getting 300,000 IU of Vitamin D were 5X less likely to die – Sept 2021

      • Vitamin D and COVID, review of evidence, loading dose if less than 50 ng - Masterjohn Sept 2021

      Pre COVID

      • Vitamin D loading dose (stoss therapy) proven to improve health

      • Half as many problems if take Vitamin D (300,000 IU) before thyroidectomy – RCT Jan 2021

      • Fewer days in hospital if 300,000 IU of vitamin D before brain surgery - RCT Feb 2021

      • 18 fewer hospital days if given 500,000 IU of vitamin D while ventilated in ICU – RCT June 2016
