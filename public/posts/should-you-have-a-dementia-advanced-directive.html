      Should you have a dementia advanced directive

      January 19, 2018

      One Day Your Mind May Fade. At Least You’ll Have a Plan New York Times

      • “lifetime risk at age 70 was 30.8 percent for men and 37.4 percent for women.”

      https://www.dementia-directive.org/

      Basically asks the following 3 questions for mild, moderate and severe stages of dementia

      1. To live for as long as I could. I would want full efforts to prolong my life, including efforts to restart my heart if it stops beating.

      2. To only receive care in the place where I am living. I would not want to go to the hospital even if I were very ill.

      3. To receive comfort-oriented care only, focused on relieving my suffering such as pain, anxiety, or breathlessness
