      Vitamin D and Omega-3 are the first focus of Global Nutrition and Health Alliance

      September 18, 2014

      http://www.globalnutritionhealth.org/ Vitamin D/Omega-3 Announced Sept 2014

      The Global Nutrition & Health Alliance was formed to help educate consumers and healthcare professionals around the world about optimal nutrition, including the use of vitamin, minerals and supplements (VMS) as part of a healthy lifestyle.

      By providing unified scientific evidence, the Alliance hopes to bring greater clarity and understanding about the challenges of achieving optional nutrition through diet alone.

      Objectives

      1. Maintain a balanced, global, multi-disciplinary group of physicians and nutrition experts to identify pressing challenges in nutrition.

      2. Educate professionals and the public about optimal nutrition as part of a healthy lifestyle

      3. Contribute and interpret evolving science surrounding appropriate use of VMS in the context of diet.

      4. Establish GNHA as “go-to” reputable, multi-disciplinary resource for consumers, healthcare professionals, media and industry peers.

      Funding: Educational grant from Reckitt Benckiser

      Reckitt Benckiser plc is a multinational consumer goods company headquartered in Slough, Berkshire, England.
