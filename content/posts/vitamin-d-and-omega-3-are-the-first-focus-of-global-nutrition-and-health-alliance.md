### [http://www.globalnutritionhealth.org/](http://www.globalnutritionhealth.org/)  Vitamin D/Omega-3 Announced Sept 2014

 **The Global Nutrition & Health Alliance was formed to help educate consumers and healthcare professionals around the world about optimal nutrition, including the use of vitamin, minerals and supplements (VMS) as part of a healthy lifestyle.** 

 **By providing unified scientific evidence, the Alliance hopes to bring greater clarity and understanding about the challenges of achieving optional nutrition through diet alone.** 

#### Objectives

1. Maintain a balanced, global, multi-disciplinary group of physicians and nutrition experts to identify pressing challenges in nutrition.

1. Educate professionals and the public about optimal nutrition as part of a healthy lifestyle

1. Contribute and interpret evolving science surrounding appropriate use of VMS in the context of diet.

1. Establish GNHA as "go-to" reputable, multi-disciplinary resource for consumers, healthcare professionals, media and industry peers.
