The conference "Vitamin D and Health in the 21st Century: An Update" was convened September 5-6, 2007, by the NIH Office of Dietary Supplements, bringing together scientists with many areas of expertise. Conference proceedings are published in the August 2008 supplement to the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition, published by the American Society for Nutrition. Below are links to the 20 articles from this issue. For more information on the publication, see the NIH News Release.
