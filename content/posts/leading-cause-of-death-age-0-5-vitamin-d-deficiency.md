### Deaths 0-5 which are associated with low vitamin D

| | | |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Health Problem | % of deaths | Reference in VitaminDWiki |
| Preterm Birth complications  | 15% |  |
| Pneumonia | 13% | ((Largest cause of infant deaths is respiratory infections, which is associated with low vitamin D – April 2011 | RTI and low vitamin D)) |
| Diarrhea | 9% | ((Rotavirus diarrhea (nearly every child gets it) is strongly associated with low vitamin D – Aug 2015 | Rotavirus diarrhea)) |
| Sepsis | 7% | ((Newborns with sepsis – 9 ng of vitamin D, without sepsis 19 ng – Aug 2015 | Sepsis newborns have half as much vitamin D)) |
