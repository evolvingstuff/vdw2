 **Study prefers 40 ng, but recommends a minimum of 30 ng** 

 **Does not appear to mention several important factors** 

* Get Vitamin D high as soon as possible - before conception is best

   * Can [restore vitamin D levels in less than a week](/posts/loading-dose-of-vitamin-d)

* Vitamin D helps the [fertility of the man as well as the woman](/posts/fertility-and-sperm)

* [Obese need > 4,000 IU to get to the same blood level](/posts/obese-need-2x-to-3x-more-vitamin-d)

* Various genes can restrict response of Vitamin D in the blood and the tissue 

* [Monthly Vitamin D may be better than daily](/posts/monthly-120000-iu-vitamin-d-plus-daily-calcium-was-great-during-pregnancies-rct) (due to genes,  less likely to forget)

* Vitamin D in oil carrier  is not appropriate for ~10% of the population

   * Vitamin D in powder or emulsion provides a better response, about the same price

* [Omega-3](/posts/pregnancy-and-infants-healthier-with-omega-3-supplementation-many-studies) Magnesium, etc helps preganaices, infants 

   * and gets more Vitamin D into both the blood and tissues
