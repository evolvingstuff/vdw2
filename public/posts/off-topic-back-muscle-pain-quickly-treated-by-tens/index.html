    Off topic: back muscle pain quickly treated by TENS

    January 25, 2023

    A few times a year a tiny portion of my back would seize up making movement and walking painful

    In April 2022 I purchased a $32 TENS machine from Amazon

    The next opportunity to use the TENS machine was in Jan 2023

    Pain is almost entirely gone after ~ four 30-minute treatments in a single day

    It has lots of options - but I only used the defaults on level 3 intensity out of 10

    VitaminDWiki - Back Pain category contains

    TENS and backpain publications

    • THE EFFECTS OF TRANSCUTANEOUS ELECTRICAL NERVE STIMULATION (TENS) USING DIFFERENT PARAMETERS AND SITE PLACEMENTS ON PERCEPTION OF PAIN IN CHRONIC LOW BACK PAIN PATIENTS - 2022 dissertation. TENS appears to generate natural opiates - http://hdl.handle.net/10106/30280 FREE PDF

    • Using TENS for Pain Control: Update on the State of the Evidence - https://doi.org/10.3390/medicina58101332 - Sept 2022 - appears to help FREE PDF

    • Characterising the Features of 381 Clinical Studies Evaluating Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) for Pain Relief: A Secondary Analysis of the Meta-TENS Study to Improve Future Research - June 2022 https://doi.org/10.3390/medicina58060803 FREE PDF

    • Does TENS Reduce the Intensity of Acute and Chronic Pain? A Comprehensive Appraisal of the Characteristics and Outcomes of 169 Reviews and 49 Meta-Analyses - Oct 2021 https://doi.org/10.3390/medicina57101060

      • “Therefore, we recommend that TENS should be considered as a treatment option.”

    In May 2023 the US FDA approved implant nerve stimulation to relieve chronic long-term backpain
