#### [Dr Miriam Stoppard: Why dementia gets more severe in the winter](https://www.mirror.co.uk/lifestyle/dr-miriam-stoppard-dementia-gets-13850545) UK Mirror

* “A study found a 30% increase in the likelihood of a person reaching the threshold for dementia during the winter months”

* “It found people with dementia were mentally more alert in summer and autumn and, crucially, mental function declined by the equivalent of  **nearly five years**  in the winter and spring months”
