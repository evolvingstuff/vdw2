#### First Ecuadorian statement consensus for the evaluation and treatment of osteoporosis

Archives of Osteoporosis volume 18, Article number: 81 (2023) https://doi.org/10.1007/s11657-023-01263-5

Carlos Rios, Genessis Maldonado, Sara Vargas, José González, Claudia Vera, Andrés Zuñiga, José Martínez, Mayra Castillo, Raúl Jervis, Rosa Ventura, Sergio Guevara, Gabriela Torres, Franklín Uguña, Osvaldo Daniel Messina, José Luis Neyro, Daniel Fernández, Roberto Guerrero & Mario Moreno 

<img src="/attachments/d3.mock.jpg" alt="image" width="1100">

Summary

Osteoporosis management has become more relevant as the life expectancy increases. In Ecuador, approximately 19% of adults over 65 years of age have been diagnosed with osteoporosis. There is no national consensus for the management and prevention of the disease being this proposal the first Ecuadorian consensus.
