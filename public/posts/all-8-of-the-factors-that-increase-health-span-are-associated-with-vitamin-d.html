      Not smoking

          Stopping smoking increases Vitamin D

      Regular physical activity

          Vitamin D is increased by intense exercise

         More Vitamin D makes exercise easier

      Healthy weight

         Good Vitamin D level reduces weight gain

         Weight reduction efforts lose more if Vitamin D is added

      Healthy diet

          Vitamin D partially compensates for a poor diet

      Healthy sleep (average of 7-9 hours)

          Vitamin D improves sleep

      Blood pressure in a healthy range

          Vitamin D reduces hypertension

      Blood glucose in a healthy range

          Vitamin D both prevents and treats diabetes

      Non-HDL cholesterol in a healthy range

          Vitamin D reduces non-HDL cholesterol
