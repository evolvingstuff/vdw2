      The many topics not discussed include

      1. % activation of vitamin D in the cells rather than in the liver & kidneys

      2. When are loading doses better than daily

      3. The many months to plateau the response to a new supplementation level

      4. Efficacy of other forms for Vitamin D, such as nanoemulsion

        • Including gut-friendly forms of vitamin D

      5. Higher Vitamin D thresholds for some diseases

      6. Some diseases reduce the levels of Vitamin D in the blood

      7. Risk of Toxicity can be substantially reduce by adjusting cofactors

        • such as reduce calcium, increase Vitamin K2

      8. Microbiome interaction with Vitamin D

      9. 4X range of response between individuals to both supplementation and UVB
