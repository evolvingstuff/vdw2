      Vitamin D and COVID-19 - observational studies found it helps, never hurts - Campbell

      November 1, 2020

      Winter Vitamin D Update 27minute video

      Dr. John Campbell (UK) has produced daily evidence-based videos on COVID-19 since ~ Feb 2020
      About 4 of his videos have focused on Vitamin D
       
      He apparently saved COVID-19 information into 3 sub-categories
          1) Helps, 2) Does not appear to help, 3) Hurts
      So far Vitamin D has been often observed to help, occasionally not help, but never observed to hurt
       
      While he is taking 5X the minimum amount recommended by the UK govt (2,000 IU),
          which is unlikely NOT enough to fight COVID-19.
