      Whereas the Coimbra protocol uses far more: 20,000 to 140,000 IU of Vitamin D daily

      • Coimbra does not do randomized controlled trials – for the following reasons
      1. The benefit is given to all with MS (or other autoimmune diseases) - not just the half getting the Vitamin D
      1. The Vitamin D dose size varies by 7X with the individual - not one dose-size fits all
      1. The protocol is not monotherapy (contains more than just Vitamin D)
      1. Vitamin D doses are so high as to cause hypercalcaemia if Calcium were not restricted and lots of water prescribed
