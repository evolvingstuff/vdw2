      Rare diseases: 1 in 10 Americans (some are helped by Vitamin D) - 2023

      September 29, 2023

      There are more than 10,000 known rare diseases.

      Approximately 80% of rare diseases are estimated to have a genetic origin.

      Of those, 70% start in childhood. (56% of rare diseases start in childhood)

      • “In the United States, about half of the people with a rare disease are children. Many of these diseases are chronic and worsen over time.”

      The US defines incidence of a “rare disease” as being 1 in every 1,500 people

      The founder of VitaminDWiki has seen many “rare” diseases fought by Vitamin D, but had not posted them since so few people would be interested in each disease
