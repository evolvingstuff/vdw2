* Significantly higher rates of hospitalization due to non-C0VID respiratory illness in the vaxxed

* Even higher numbers in those who were double vaxxed

Appears to refer to: [Monitoring reports of the effectiveness of COVID-19 vaccination](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/monitoring-reports-of-the-effectiveness-of-covid-19-vaccination) July 20, 2023
