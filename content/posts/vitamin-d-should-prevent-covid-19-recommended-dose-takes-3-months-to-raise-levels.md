#### Optimisation of Vitamin D Status for Enhanced Immuno-protection Against Covid-19

Issue: Ir Med J; Vol 113; No. 4; P58

D.M. McCartney1, D.G. Byrne2,3

They appear to recommend 800 to 2000 IU

In three months this will get  vitamin  levels >20 ng/ml

COVID-19 appears to need levels >40 ng

Suspect fewer than 10% of the population taking 2000 IU will get > 40ng/ml in 3 months
