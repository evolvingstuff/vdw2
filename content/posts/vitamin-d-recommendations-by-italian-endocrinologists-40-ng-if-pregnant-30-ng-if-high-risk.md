* Dark skin

* Wear concealing clothing

* People who get very little noon-day sun

* Diabetic

* On dialysis

* Multiple Sclerosis

* Gut problems

* Gallbladder removed

* HIV

* Depression

* Anemia

* Little Magnesium in water

* Take drugs which remove Magnesium from Body

* Drink > 1 can of soft drink daily

* Autistic

* Breastfeeding

* Smoker or both parents smoke
