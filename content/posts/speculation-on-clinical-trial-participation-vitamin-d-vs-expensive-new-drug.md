#### Speculation: Randomized Controlled Trials for wonder drugs and Vitamin D have very different participations

#### Participation varies with  
&nbsp; &nbsp; 1) How much the person is paid,  
&nbsp; &nbsp;  2) The cost/availability of the drug  
&nbsp; &nbsp; 3) The belief that it will help
