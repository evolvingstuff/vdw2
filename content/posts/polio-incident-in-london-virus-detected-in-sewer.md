#### Britain Declares National Incident After Poliovirus Found in London - June 22, 2022

 **[NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2022/06/22/health/uk-polio-london-poliovirus.html)** 

 **[<i class="fas fa-file-pdf" style="margin-right: 0.3em;"></i>NYT PDF ](/attachments/d3.mock.pdf)** 

* "The last case of polio in Britain was in 1984, and the country was declared polio-free in 2003. Before the introduction of the polio vaccine, epidemics were common in Britain, with up to 8,000 cases of paralysis reported every year."

* "Routine surveillance of sewage in the country picks up poliovirus once or twice a year, but between February and May, officials identified the virus in several samples collected in London,..."

* “Most of the disease is asymptomatic, it is only about one in 500 children who are actually paralyzed,”
