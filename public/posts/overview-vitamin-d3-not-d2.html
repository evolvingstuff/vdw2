      Overview Vitamin D3 not D2

      October 31, 2011

      Vitamin D2 has been known for a long time to not be as good as vitamin D3

      Vitamin D2 was the first to be discovered and, until 2011, was the only one that most doctors could prescribe

      The D3 form has been known for many decades, but the medical profession has not yet adapted - after all, only 12,000 papers on D3 asof Oct 2023.

      Several recent studies have shown that vitamin D2 actually reduces the levels of D3 in the blood

      AND, worse still, D2 actually INCREASES a health problem , instead of reducing it, the way vitamin D3 does

      Notes:

      Some multivitamins and milk/food fortifications still use D2 , but in such low quantities that it can be ignored.

      Some people buy D2 because it is vegan, being unaware that there are vegan forms of D3
