*  **[Menstrual Cycle Changes After COVID Vaccine Are Temporary](https://www.webmd.com/women/pms/news/20220127/any-change-to-menstrual-cycle-after-covid-vaccine-is-minor-temporary-studies%20) WebMD Jan 27, 2022**
