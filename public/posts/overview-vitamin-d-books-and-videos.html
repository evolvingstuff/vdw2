      Books as of Nov 2012 -added a few in April 2014

      • Prevent many diseases = List of many books some good ones include”

        • Vitamin D Solution – Holick 2010 author is one of the top 5 researchers, pretty good, many libraries will have the book

        • Power Of Vitamin D - Zaidi 2010 - ebook is $3 and free to Amazon Prime customers easy to read

      • Healthy in Seven Days - Loading dose of Vitamin D – book 2014

      • Vitamin D- Miracle Cure - Mudd 2011 very low cost, easy read, edited by Henry Lahore (founder of VitaminDWiki)

      • Prescribing Sunshine - book Aug 2012

      This is an excellent first book on vitamin D. It is well written, with memorable stories.

      • Vitamin D - Is This The Miracle Vitamin – book Aug 2012 New Zealand, investigative reporter

      • Vitamin D Simplified - book May 2012 author is a person who benefited from vitamin D $3

      • Athletics and Vitamin D – Cannell 2011 excellent on Athletics, covers vitamin D concerns well.

      • Prevent or treat a specific disease = a few books

        • Diabetes – Zaidi   Diabetes - Hurley

        • Autism low-cost e-book

        • Breast Cancer 2010

        • Dental a small section on vitamin D

        • Bone Health

      • Reduce aches and pains

        • Back pain cured with vitamin D – Book May 2014

        • Pregnancy and vitamin D 2010
