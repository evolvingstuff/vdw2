##### [Older Women Who Exercise Outdoors More Likely to Stick with It](http://www.scientificamerican.com/article/older-women-who-exercise-outdoorsscientific%20American%20Dec%202014-more-likely-to-stick-with-it/) Scientific American

3 month trial – 3 days per week of 1 hour postmenopausal women

Outdoor exercise 97% continued

Indoor exercise 91% continued

##### From [Adherence to exercise and affective responses: comparison between outdoor and indoor training.](http://journals.lww.com/menopausejournal/Abstract/publishahead/Adherence_to_exercise_and_affective_responses__.98266.aspx) Menopause Nov 2014

 **For only the outdoor group** 

Decreased Depression symptoms 

Increased physical activity level
