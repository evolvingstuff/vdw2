 **<i class="fas fa-file-pdf" style="margin-right: 0.3em;"></i><a href="https://d1bk1kqxc0sym.cloudfront.net/attachments/pdf/long-haul-nyt-feb-2022-compressed.pdf">Download the NYT PDF from VitaminDWiki</a>** 

Excellent look at the science

 **Here are their references** 

— [Survivor Corps](https://www.survivorcorps.com/) keeps a [directory of post-Covid clinics](https://www.survivorcorps.com/pccc).

— Dysautonomia International offers a [list of doctors](http://www.dysautonomiainternational.org/page.php?ID=14) with experience treating autonomic disorders commonly seen in long Covid.

— [Body Politic](https://www.wearebodypolitic.com/covid-19) hosts a Covid support group where thousands of long haulers share [information](https://www.wearebodypolitic.com/resources) and advice on Slack.

— The [Long Covid Support Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/longcovid/) hosts a community on Facebook.

— The Royal College of Occupational Therapists offers advice for [managing post-Covid fatigue](https://www.rcot.co.uk/how-manage-post-viral-fatigue-after-covid-19-0).

— An essay from Maria Farrell offers advice on how to get well, and the importance of [making time to rest](https://crookedtimber.org/2020/05/18/indefinitely-ill-post-covid-fatigue/).

— Americans with long Covid may qualify for [disability benefits](https://www.hhs.gov/civil-rights/for-providers/civil-rights-covid19/guidance-long-covid-disability/index.html), although without conclusive medical results, many people [face roadblocks](https://www.nytimes.com/2021/10/27/us/long-covid-disability-benefits.html).

— Three leading researchers into long Covid often share information about the latest findings on Twitter: [Dr. Amy Proal](https://twitter.com/microbeminded2), a microbiologist at PolyBio Research Institute; [Dr. David Putrino](https://twitter.com/PutrinoLab), the director of rehabilitation innovation for the Mount Sinai Health System; and [Dr. Iwasaki](https://twitter.com/VirusesImmunity), the Yale immunologist.

— [Health Rising](https://www.healthrising.org/) covers the latest research into long Covid, ME/CFS and other chronic illnesses in detail.

— Gez Medinger, a video producer, interviews some [prominent researchers](https://www.youtube.com/c/RUNDMC1/videos) into long Covid on YouTube.

— A [video interview](https://youtu.be/ZOR1250ouIo) with Dr. Svetlana Blitshteyn, a neurologist and the director of the Dysautonomia Clinic, offers advice for treatment and an overview of current research into autonomic disorders.

— A [detailed guide](https://www.dysautonomiainternational.org/pdf/RoweOIsummary.pdf) to understanding, treating and living with orthostatic intolerance is available from the Johns Hopkins Children’s Center.
