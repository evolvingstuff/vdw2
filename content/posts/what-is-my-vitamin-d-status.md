### [What is my vitamin D status?](https://www.boombox.com/c/quiz/120143/f70f9ff9-129e-4eb4-b8c5-165636f84432?mc_cid=f0fa36dbf8&mc_eid=1f9eb8e0b6) Vitamin D Council Oct 2015

---

| | |
| --- | --- |
| Symptoms | Do you have pain in your muscles, bones, or joints or fatigue? |
| Supplementation | Do you supplement with at least 30,000 IU/week? |
| Medication use | Do you take glucocorticoids (steroid hormones used to dampen immune activity, such as Prednisone)? |
| Sun exposure | Do you get at least 30 minutes of midday (between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM) sun exposure every day without wearing sunscreen? |
| Age | Are you older than 50? |
| Diet | Do you eat wild, oily fish (salmon, mackerel, herring, sardines) at least 3 days a week? |
| Location | Do you live within 35*N (north of Atlanta and Los Angeles) and 35*S (south of Sydney, Australia, or Buenos Aires, Argentina)? |
| Body weight | Is your BMI greater than 30? |
