#### 2013 IBI report had a cost breakdown which included Presenteeism

 **Based on $514/employee average** 

| | |
| --- | --- |
| Sick days:  | $131 |
|  **Presenteeism:**  | $83 |
| Short-term disability | $71 |
| Long-term disability | $42 |
| Workers’ compensation | $19 |

 

* "Employees with back pain are absent four more days per year than those without this ailment 

   * and have the equivalent of 4.4 more days of presenteeism annually. "

* The cost of less work time and presenteeism makes up more than two-thirds of the total cost of low-back pain to employers, according to IBI, a workforce health and productivity research and measurement organization
