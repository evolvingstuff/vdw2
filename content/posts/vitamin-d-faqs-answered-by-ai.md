A portion of the reply

Calcium carbonate, a common form of calcium supplement often derived from rocks, has a range of potential side effects. 

These include 

* high calcium levels, 

* low phosphate levels, 

* vomiting, 

* nausea, 

* constipation, 

* gas, 

* loss of appetite or anorexia, 

* irritability,
