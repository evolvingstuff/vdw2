#### Use of Dietary Vitamin Supplements and Risk of Thyroid Cancer - April 2017

* use of multivitamins (OR =  **** , 95 % CI: 0.33, 1.04) and 

> calcium supplementation (OR = **0.45** , 95 % CI: 0.22, 0.93).
