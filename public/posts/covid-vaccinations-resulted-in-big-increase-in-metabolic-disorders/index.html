    COVID vaccinations resulted in big increase in metabolic disorders

    November 22, 2023

    Metabolic Disorders Post-COVID-19┬аVaccinations

    TrialSite News

    “While┬а[cardiovascular disorders](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37973371/; https://www.trialsitenews.com/a/are-covid-19-vaccine-induced-adverse-cardiovascular-events-rare-01b2657e), cancers (link#1;┬аlink#2), immune system disorders (link#1;┬аlink#2), and neurological disorders (link#1;┬аlink#2) post-COVID-19 vaccination have been studied to a modest extent, metabolic disorders following COVID-19 vaccination have not been studied to the same extent.┬а┬а"

    • “To obtain the VAERS results for post-COVID-19 vaccination metabolic disorders, the final list of ~3100 MedDRA LLT terms (see Appendix 1 for the specific MedDRA query used to identify metabolic disorder-related symptoms in VAERS) was intersected with all the ~18,000 VAERS terms, resulting in 335 VAERS symptoms (with non-zero entries) related to metabolic disorders post-COVID-19 vaccination (see Appendix 2 for the VAERS COVID-19 results).
