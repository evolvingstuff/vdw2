## While there are many good points in the book, and it is very up to date, but there are also a great many errors - which were

* Misleading

* Incorrect

* Failures to mention information

* Internally inconsistent

## The following details the errors found in first 2 hours of reading.

* Note:  **T = T** op of page, &nbsp; &nbsp; **M = M** iddle of page, &nbsp; &nbsp; **B = B** ottom of page, &nbsp; &nbsp; **X**  not mentioned on page

Pg 20 –  Incorrect: extrapolated Canadian lives saved per year to the US, apparently without noticing that US has more UVB than Canada

Pg 24B – Inconsistent: >50 ng/ml is toxic – yet page 30 said that 60 ng/ml is typical when have lots of time in the sun

Pg 33M – Not mentioned: 50,000 IU – as prescribed in the US is virtually always vitamin D **2** , not D3

Pg 33M – Not mentioned: 50,000 IU prescription is also twice, or three times a week, not just the once a week mentioned

Pg 46B – Source of the information, IoM, was incorrect: Must avoid excessive Calcium if have >2,000 IU of vitamin D. 

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Many studies have shown that people [should not have](/tags/should-not-have.html) > 750 mg of Calcium if >2,000 IU of vitamin D

Pg 73M – Misleading: 600 IU of vitamin D needed to detect effect on falls. [Fails to mention](/tags/fails-to-mention.html) that 2,000 – 4,000 IU provides excellent benefits

Pg 74M – Not mentioned: There are many other forms of Calcium which are much more tolerated and bio-available.

Pg 85B – Misleading: Studies have shown a [5X increase of getting TB is low on vitamin D](/tags/5x-increase-of-getting-tb-is-low-on-vitamin-d.html)

Pg 87M – Not mentioned: [Many studies](/tags/many-studies.html) have shown great reduction in flu etc, with vitamin D

Pg 90M – Incorrect: Many MS studies have shown a [strong latitude effect](/tags/strong-latitude-effect.html) in North America and elsewhere

Pg 108X – Not Mentioned: [Meta-analysis of colon cancer and vitamin D](/tags/meta-analysis-of-colon-cancer-and-vitamin-d.html) has already shown  

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;for every 100 IU of vitamin D the rate of colon cancer decreases by 4%. We do not have to wait 5 years for the VITAL study results

Pg 122X – Not Mentioned: Vitamin D also [prevents many cancers](/tags/prevents-many-cancers.html)

Pg 122X – Not Mentioned: Vitamin D aids [some chemotherapies so much that it can cause the patient to die](/tags/some-chemotherapies-so-much-that-it-can-cause-the-patient-to-die.html) – must be very careful

Pg 122X – Not Mentioned: [Vitamin D the only supplement which consistently helps cancer survivors](/tags/vitamin-d-the-only-supplement-which-consistently-helps-cancer-survivors.html)

Pg 147X – Not mentioned: Vitamin D can [TREAT as well as prevent diabetes](/tags/treat-as-well-as-prevent-diabetes.html)

Pg 189 – Misleading: [Times in the sun given are for thin, white, young people who are laying down](/tags/times-in-the-sun-given-are-for-thin-white-young-people-who-are-laying-down.html) 

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Thin, white, young people who are STANDING UP, need at 10-20 minutes.

Pg 202T – Misleading: Farmed salmon has far less vitamin D – this is alluded to several pages later, however

Pg 208M – Not Mentioned – there are [other forms of vitamin D for people who cannot adsorb fats or have poor bowel absorption.](/tags/other-forms-of-vitamin-d-for-people-who-cannot-adsorb-fats-or-have-poor-bowel-absorption.html)

Pg 211M – Incorrect: Cannot get enough vitamin D from the sun walking for a few minutes in the sun three times a week. [CLICK HERE](/tags/click-here.html)  

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Probably need 40-80 minutes to get to 36 ng/ml of vitamin D

Pg 212M – Incorrect: “Most healthy adults only need 600-800 IU a day”  This is not [nearly enough for “healthy”](/tags/nearly-enough-for-healthy.html)

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;elderly, pregnant women, people with dark skin, high latitude, avoid sun for religious reasons, concern about skin cancer, stay indoors for video, etc.  

&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;See also the [11 new reasons for vitamin D deficiency](/tags/11-new-reasons-for-vitamin-d-deficiency.html)

Pg 214T – Misleading: Fails to mention many [more forms of Calcium](/tags/more-forms-of-calcium.html) – which are more bio-available and easier to digest

Pg 218T – Not Mentioned: Need not take vitamin D every day.  The body stores vitamin D up nicely. [Can just take it weekly if you wish](/tags/can-just-take-it-weekly-if-you-wish.html)

Pg 219M – Not Mentioned:Additional [conditions which increase your need for vitamin D](/tags/conditions-which-increase-your-need-for-vitamin-d.html) include MS,  recent surgery, Colon cancer, Bariatric surgery, Crohn’s disease and Celiac Disease

Pg 222T – Misleading: A few men get enough vitamin D from foods, [but virtually no women get enough](/tags/but-virtually-no-women-get-enough.html)

Pg 222M – Inconsistent: Some evidence that internal absorption of vitamin D is reduced in the elderly.  (which is true) but stated just the opposite earlier in the book.

Pg 229T – Misleading: Yes. pregnant women and nursing mothers ‘need’ 600 IU of vitamin D – [but they actually need 4000 to 6000 IU](/tags/but-they-actually-need-4000-to-6000-iu.html)

Pg 241T – Incorrect: Studies have shown [adverse reactions to vitamin D above 15,000 IU for a long period of time](/tags/adverse-reactions-to-vitamin-d-above-15000-iu-for-a-long-period-of-time.html)  Example  50,000 IU for 2 months. 

Pg 244M – Misleading: [Tanning salons are probably safe](/tags/tanning-salons-are-probably-safe.html) – if you are not in them for very long.
