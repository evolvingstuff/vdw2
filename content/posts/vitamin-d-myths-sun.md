   *  **Nope - only if [12 conditions are true](/posts/no-10-minutes-per-day-of-sun-uvb-is-not-enough):&nbsp; i.e. under 40, summer, white skin, lying down, little clothing** 

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Same amount of vitamin D generated in each portion of skin</span>**  

   *  **Nope - for example, [8X more Vitamin D generated by hands and face](/posts/vitamin-d-produced-by-hands-and-face-is-8x-more-than-elsewhere)** 

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">No Vitamin D from the winter sun</span>**  

   *  **Nope - [winter can provide 500 IU Vitamin D](/posts/vitamin-d-from-the-sun-during-northern-winters-contrary-to-current-belief)**

   *  **Nope - low exposure will INCREASE total cancer by 50X** 

*  **<span style="color:#F00;">Same IU of Vitamin D to everyone from the sun</span>**  

   *  **Nope - 4X difference between individuals,** 

      *  **without considering additional  differences due to skin color, age, obesity, etc.**

   *  **Nope - and sunscreen often INCREASES Skin cancer by being in sun too long**
