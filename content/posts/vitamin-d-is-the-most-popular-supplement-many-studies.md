* “Retail analysts Mintel said 56 per cent of those who use supplements take a multivitamin. 

> Of those who take a single supplement, 

> &nbsp; &nbsp; 33 % take vitamin D, 

> &nbsp; &nbsp; 27 % vitamin C, 

> &nbsp; &nbsp; 15 % vitamin B, 

> &nbsp; &nbsp; 12 %vitamin A and
