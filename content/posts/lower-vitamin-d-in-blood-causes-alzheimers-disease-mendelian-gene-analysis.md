J Alzheimers Dis. 2019 Dec 2. [doi: 10.3233/JAD-190713](https://doi.org/10.3233/JAD-190713)

Wang L1, Qiao Y2, Zhang H3, Zhang Y2, Hua J4, Jin S4, Liu G3,5.
