      5-10 minutes of sun provides ONLY 1,000 IU ONLY IF

      • you are near the equator

      • AND young (seniors 4X longer)

      • AND not obese ( need 3X longer)

      • AND have light skin (Dark Skin need 4X longer)

      • AND it is summer

      • AND it is the middle of the day

      • AND you have lots of skin exposed to the sun

      • AND you are lying down

      • AND you are not wearing sunscreen

      • AND you have a healthy Kidney

      • AND there are no clouds nor air pollution

      • AND you have a good response to sunshine (4X variation between individuals)
