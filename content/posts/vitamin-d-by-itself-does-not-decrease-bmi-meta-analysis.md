#### Vitamin D supplementation and body fat mass: a systematic review and meta-analysis

European Journal of Clinical Nutrition (2018), doi:10.1038/s41430-018-0132-z

Mahdieh Golzarand, Bruce W. Hollis, Parvin Mirmiran, Carol L. Wagner & Sakineh Shab-Bidar

<div class="border" style="background-color:#FFFAE2;padding:15px;margin:10px 0;border-radius:5px;width:700px">

 **Increasing Vitamin D reduces fat if any of the following are also included  
 &nbsp; Add Calcium  
 &nbsp; Add Exercise  
 &nbsp; Calorie reduction  
 &nbsp; Add Magnesium  
 &nbsp; Decrease Omega-6   
 &nbsp; Increase Omega-3** 

 **Also: Vitamin D reduces weight gain**
