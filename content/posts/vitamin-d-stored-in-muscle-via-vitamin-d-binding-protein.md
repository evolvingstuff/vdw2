#### The Role of Skeletal Muscle in Maintaining Vitamin D Status in Winter

Current Developments in Nutrition, nzz087, https://doi.org/10.1093/cdn/nzz087

<div class="border" style="background-color:#FFFAE2;padding:15px;margin:10px 0;border-radius:5px;width:700px">

* This study speculates that sem-iactivated Vitamin D is stored in muscle tissue via VDBP

* Some appears to be released during exercise and when the PTH level increases

* It does not consider the possibility of storage of semi-activated and/or non-activated Vitamin D in other body tissues
