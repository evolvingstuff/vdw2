Methods: Seventy-one volunteers with hypovitaminosis D were randomly assigned to 2 dose regimens: cholecalciferol 50 000 IU weekly for 8 weeks, then 50 000 IU monthly for 2 months (group A ) and 7000 IU daily for 8 weeks, then 12 500 IU weekly for 2 months (group B ).
