      Vitamin D educational materials needed

      March 26, 2012

      People need to learn about vitamin D

      • UK survey of vitamin D knowledge of midwives - 2011

      • Just 15 minutes of education improved vitamin D compliance – Mar 2012

      • 28 % of dark skinned not heard of vitamin D and 54 % not know symptoms – Jan 2012

      • Very poor knowledge of vitamin D deficiency, even after diagnosis in Kuwait - March 2012 highlights - only:

      Only 28% of participants were aware of their condition,

      Only 12% of the participants knew that the level of vitamin D dropped again after stopping medication.

      Only 29% knew the relation between vitamin D and other diseases.

      Information about vitamin D is changing rapidly

      • 4,000+ peer-reviewed vitamin D paper expected in 2012

      • A static publication on vitamin D becomes somewhat obsolete in a year

      • The VitaminDWiki can automatically bubble vitamin D updates to custom publications
