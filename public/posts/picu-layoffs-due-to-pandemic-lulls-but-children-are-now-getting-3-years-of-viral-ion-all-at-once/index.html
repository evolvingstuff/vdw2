      New Hybrid Virus Discovered as Flu And RSV Fuse Into Single Pathogen - Oct 31. 2022

      Science Allerts

      • “he hybrid virus looks like a gecko’s foot under the microscope, with respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) forming the legs and influenza A virus forming the toes.”

      • “When these two viruses join forces, influenza A appears to infect a higher number and broader range of human cells. The influenza A particles were found to evade the immune system by displaying the RSV surface proteins, giving the virus a survival advantage.”

      • “The hybrid also spread into cells that lacked influenza receptors, which could allow influenza A to move further down the respiratory tract into the lungs and lead to more severe infection”

      R.S.V. Cases Are Rising. Here’s What You Need to Know - New York Times
