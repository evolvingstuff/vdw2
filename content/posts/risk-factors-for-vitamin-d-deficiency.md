### Quite comprehensive, but fails to include:

* Decreased Magnesium

* Decreased Cholesterol

* More people living in cities

* Whiter skin wanted

* Skin Cancer feared

* Soft drinks

* Smoking

* Meat from factory farms

* Increased PCBs

* DDT, Roundup

* Surgery and trauma

* Chemotherapy

* Excess Polyunsaturated fats

* Have a disease which decreases vitamin D: MS, HIV, Diabetes, 

* Genes

* Statins (most)
