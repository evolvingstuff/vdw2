      J Diabetes Res. 2024 Sep 10:2024: doi: 10.1155/2024/9960656.

      Maryam Afraie 1, Pourya Bahrami 1, Parisa Kohnepoushi 1, Sorour Khateri 2, Lobat Majidi 2, Lotfollah Saed 3, Kamran Zamani 1, Hedyeh Mohammadi Baharm 1, Yousef Moradi 4, Farhad Moradpour 4
