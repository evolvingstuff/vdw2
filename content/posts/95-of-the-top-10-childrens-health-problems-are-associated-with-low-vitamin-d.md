Based on the prevalence data, here are the most common vision problems in children, ordered by percentage of incidence:

1. Refractive Errors (Most Common):

> Hyperopia (Farsightedness): 21% in children 6 months to 6 years [|PDF] Children's Vision and Eye Health: A Snapshot of Current National ..."  [1](https://preventblindness.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/Snapshot-Report-2020condensedF.pdf)

> Astigmatism: 15-28% in children 5-17 years [|PDF] Children's Vision and Eye Health: A Snapshot of Current National ..."  [1](https://preventblindness.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/Snapshot-Report-2020condensedF.pdf)

> Myopia (Nearsightedness): 4% in children 6 months to 6 years, increasing to 9% in children 5-17 years [|PDF] Children's Vision and Eye Health: A Snapshot of Current National ..." [1](https://preventblindness.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/Snapshot-Report-2020condensedF.pdf)

1. Overall Vision Disorders: 20.2% (1 in 4.9 live births) in first year of life [2](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36898661/)

1. Most Common Diagnoses in Infants:

> Conjunctivitis: 51.5% of diagnosed cases

> Nasolacrimal Duct Obstruction: 33.6%

> Pseudostrabismus: 4.1% [2](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36898661/)

1. Other Significant Conditions:

> Strabismus (Misaligned eyes): 2-4% in children under 6 years [3](https://preventblindness.org/common-childrens-vision-problems-prevalence/)

> Amblyopia (Lazy eye): 2-4% in young children [|PDF] Children's Vision and Eye Health: A Snapshot of Current National ..." [1](https://preventblindness.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/Snapshot-Report-2020condensedF.pdf) [3](https://preventblindness.org/common-childrens-vision-problems-prevalence/)

> Visual Impairment/Blindness: Nearly 3% of children under 18 years [3](https://preventblindness.org/common-childrens-vision-problems-prevalence/) [4](https://iowa.preventblindness.org/prevalence-and-impact-of-vision-disorders-in-u-s-children/)

1. Rare Conditions:

> Congenital/Pediatric Glaucoma: 0.0023% (2.3 in 100,000 newborns) [7](https://www.optometrists.org/childrens-vision/guide-to-pediatric-eye-conditions/a-guide-to-childrens-eye-diseases/)

> Retinoblastoma: 0.006% (1 in 14,000-18,000 live births) [7](https://www.optometrists.org/childrens-vision/guide-to-pediatric-eye-conditions/a-guide-to-childrens-eye-diseases/)
