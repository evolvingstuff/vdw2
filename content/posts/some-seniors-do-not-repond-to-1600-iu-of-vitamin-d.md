## Possible reasons for non-response include, but are not limited to, the following

* Lacking <a href="/posts/co-factors" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This link has an unknown page_id: 1270">co-factors</a> Magnesium, etc.

* [Surgery or other trauma](https://www.VitaminDWiki.com/tiki-browse_categories.php?parentId=64&sort_mode=created_desc) during the year decreased vitamin D levels

> <a href="/posts/taking-drugs-by-seniors-was-associated-with-about-2-ng-less-vitamin-d" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This link has an unknown page_id: 2619">Taking drugs by seniors was associated with about 2 ng less vitamin D – March 2012</a>

* <a href="/posts/taking-drugs" style="color: red; text-decoration: underline;" title="This link has an unknown page_id: 784">Taking drugs</a> such as Statins, Antiseizure, Prednisone, Orlistat, Questran, Dilatin, Phenobarbital, and Rifampin,

* [Non-alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease  (4 in 10 seniors) and Vitamin D ](/posts/non-alcoholic-fatty-liver-disease-4-in-10-seniors-and-vitamin-d)

* [People with gut problems are low on vitamin D](/posts/people-with-gut-problems-are-low-on-vitamin-d)

* Poor [kidney](/categories/kidney) function or poor endocrine function – to make the active form of vitamin D
