#### Many Post-Covid Patients Are Experiencing New Medical Problems, Study Finds - June 15, 2021

 **[NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2021/06/15/health/covid-long-haulers.html)** 

* one month or more after their infection, 23 % sought medical treatment for new conditions.

* nearly half of patients who were hospitalized for Covid-19 

* 27 percent of people who had mild or moderate symptoms 

* 19 percent of people who said they were asymptomatic.

   * “…the fact that asymptomatic people can have post-Covid symptoms is important to emphasize…”
