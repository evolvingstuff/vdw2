### [The myopia boom](http://www.nature.com/news/the-myopia-boom-1.17120#/eye%20) Nature April 2015

 **Short-sightedness is reaching epidemic proportions. Some scientists think they have found a reason why.** 

They claim decreased sunlight, rather than decreased Vitamin D

* “In Seoul, a whopping 96.5% of 19-year-old men are short-sighted.”

* “Other parts of the world have also seen a dramatic increase in the condition, which now affects around half of young adults in the United States and Europe — double the prevalence of half a century ago”
