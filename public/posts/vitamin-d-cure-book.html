      February 10, 2010

      Vitamin D Cure May 2009 by James Dowd Amazon allows you to search the text inside of the $10 book.

      Review of The Vitamin D Cure – revised edition

      Musculoskeletal pain: 93% of patients with unexplained widespread pain had vitamin D deficiency Mayo Clinic pg 12

      Cheese produces 20 points of acid per serving. 2X to 3X more than other protein sources. Dowd calls cheese the king of junk foods. Pg 23

      Magnesium deficiency: more than half of the people who are deficient in vitamin D. Need to increase it as well as vitamin D, but no easy way to test it. Pg 38

      Dowd had good results of vitamin D supplementation for fibromyalgia pg 40

      Chart of D3 dosage to get to 60 ng/ml on page 49

      if at 20 ng/ml, dose 30 IU/lb; if 10 ng/ml then dose at 38 IU/lb, if at 30ng/ml then dose at 23 IU/lb
