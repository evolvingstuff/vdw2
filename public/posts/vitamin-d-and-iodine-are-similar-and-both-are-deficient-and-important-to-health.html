      6 reasons for decrease in Iodine (resulting in a 4X decrease in 4 decades)

      1. Some countries do not require adding iodine to salt used commercially

      2. People have been minimizing use of salt to reduce sodium in their diet

      3. Iodine in salt dissapates in about 1 year

      4. People are using salt from mines, mountains that is not iodized

      5. Cow’s udders are now cleaned with Bromine instead of Iodine

      6. Iodine is no longer used in baking bread

      Switzerland has greatly INCREASED the amount of Iodine in ALL of their salt over the past 60 years

      3.75 mg/kg in 1975,

      7.5 mg/kg in 1962,

      15 mg/kg in 1980,

      20 mg/kg in 1998 vs US 70 micrograms/gram = 70 mg/kg

      Swiss fortify ALL salt, not just table salt.

      Swiss Iodine intake may be >2X of that of US.
