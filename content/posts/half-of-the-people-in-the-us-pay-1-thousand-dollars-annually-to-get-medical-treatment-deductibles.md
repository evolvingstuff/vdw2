#### [Women With Breast Cancer Delay Care When Faced With High Deductibles](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/05/04/health/breast-cancer-insurance-deductibles.html) NYT May 2018

* Study found that women with high deductibles “delayed beginning chemotherapy by an average of seven months”

* “About 11 percent of covered workers have a deductible of at least $3,000”

* Also” numerous studies show people are more likely to forgo preventive care when they have a high deductible — even if that care is free”
