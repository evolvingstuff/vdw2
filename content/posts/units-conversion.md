## The blood level of 25 (OH) Vitamin D

nanograms/milliliter (ng/ml) and nanomoles/Liter (nmol/L)

1 ng/ml = 2.5 nmol.L

30 ng/ml = 75 nmol/L

## Dose: International Units and micrograms

40 I.U. = 1 mcg

400 I.U. = 10 mcg

1000 I.U. = 25 mcg

50,000 I.U = 1250 mcg or 1.25 mg (milligram)
