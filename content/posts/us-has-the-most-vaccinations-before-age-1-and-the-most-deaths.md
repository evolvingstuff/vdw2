##### United States - 23 doses

 **[Perplexity - US, Canada, UK](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/how-many-vaccinations-ZVbA6QPrSZeHcLIoONXhxg)** 

Hepatitis B (HepB): 3 doses

Rotavirus (RV): 2-3 doses

Diphtheria, tetanus, & acellular pertussis (DTaP): 3 doses

Haemophilus influenzae type b (Hib): 2-3 doses

Pneumococcal conjugate (PCV13 or PCV15): 3-4 doses

Inactivated poliovirus (IPV): 2-3 doses

Influenza (IIV or LAIV): Yearly (for children 6 months and older)

Measles, mumps, rubella (MMR): 1 dose (at 12 months)

Varicella (VAR): 1 dose (at 12 months)

Hepatitis A (HepA): 1-2 doses, depending on the vaccine (starting at 12 months)

##### Canada - 10 doses

 **[Perplexity - US, Canada, UK](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/how-many-vaccinations-ZVbA6QPrSZeHcLIoONXhxg)** 

Diphtheria, tetanus, pertussis, polio, Haemophilus influenzae type b (DTaP-IPV-Hib): 3 doses

Pneumococcal conjugate (PCV13 or PCV15): 2-3 doses

Rotavirus (RV): 2-3 doses

Meningococcal conjugate (Men-C-C): 1 dose (at 12 months)

Measles, mumps, rubella (MMR): 1 dose (at 12 months in some provinces)

##### United Kingdom - 9 doses

 **[Perplexity - US, Canada, UK](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/how-many-vaccinations-ZVbA6QPrSZeHcLIoONXhxg)** 

6-in-1 vaccine (DTaP/IPV/Hib/HepB): 3 doses

Pneumococcal conjugate vaccine (PCV): 2 doses

Rotavirus vaccine (RV): 2 doses

MenB vaccine: 2 doses

##### Germany - 20 doses

 **[Perplexity - Germany](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/how-many-vaccinations-aQU05ouARECk.YskRmqQ9g)** 

Rotavirus vaccine: at six weeks, two months, and four months old

Diphtheria, Tetanus, and Pertussis (whooping cough) vaccine: at two, three, and four months old

Hepatitis B vaccine: at two, three, and four months old

Hib vaccine (Haemophilus influenzae type b): at two, three, and four months old

Pneumococcal (PCV) vaccine: at two and four months old

Polio vaccine: at two, three, and four months old

Measles, mumps, and rubella (MMR) vaccine: at 11–14 months old

Chickenpox (varicella) vaccine: at 11–14 months old

Men C vaccine (Meningococcal group C): one dose from 12 months old
