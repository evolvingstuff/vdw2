      Four of the 7 types of human Coronavirus only cause mild respiratory infections

      February 3, 2020

      There are seven strains of human coronaviruses: Wikipedia

      Causes Respiratory infection

      1. Human coronavirus 229E (HCoV-229E)

      2. Human coronavirus OC43 (HCoV-OC43)

      3. Human coronavirus NL63 (HCoV-NL63, New Haven coronavirus)

      4. Human coronavirus HKU1

            Note: Rhinovirus makes 10% to 40% of ”common colds”

      Causes Serious infection

      1. Severe acute respiratory syndrome-related coronavirus (SARS-CoV)

      2. Middle East respiratory syndrome-related coronavirus (MERS-CoV),

      3. Novel coronavirus (2019-nCoV) = Wuhan coronavirus.
