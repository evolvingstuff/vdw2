#### A Brief Overview  on Vitamin D for Alaska Health Care Providers

 **State of Alaska Epidemiology Bulletin, 15 pages of text, 9 pages of references** 

##### A few highlights

* Decreased fish in diet recently, especially among youth

* Decreased vitamin D levels in youth

* Decreased vitamin D levels for pregnancy and breastfeeding

* Increased rickets
