#### [What researchers learned from deliberately giving people covid](https://www.technologyreview.com/2022/02/03/1044699/what-researchers-learned-from-deliberately-giving-people-covid/?truid=e37d32694f4c44a5bda4fa26573c9726&utm_source=the_download&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=the_download.unpaid.engagement&utm_term=Active%20Qualified&utm_content=02-03-2022&mc_cid=11078e1100&mc_eid=ebe914c6fe) Technolody Review

* "The findings: The 36 volunteers, all aged 18 to 30, were exposed to a low dose of the original SARS-CoV-2 virus in the nose, the equivalent of the amount found in just a single drop of nasal fluid. Half the participants developed covid symptoms; they became infectious within just two days,"

* "Participants in the study remained infectious for an average of nine days and still had detectable levels of virus in their nose 12 days after initial exposure."
