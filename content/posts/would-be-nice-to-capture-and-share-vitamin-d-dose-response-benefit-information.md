Would be nice if captured information could be directly used by anyone: user, doctor, the pharmacist, researchers, etc. 

Patients like me does a good job for many other health concerns, but does not get into vitamin D details

http://www.patientslikeme.com

Grassroots is accumulating a database of dose/response information via a questionnaire - attached to this wiki page

Grassroots captures dose/response information along with many other things

http://www.grassrootshealth.net/

## But, Grassroots does not capture information on

BMI or height

Visiting more than three locations (in our RV we visit about 50 per year)

More than one reason to visit a hospital

On non-hospital health problems diabetes, obesity, MS. ……

Situation when going to a sunny climate but unable to be out in the sun, 

For locations close to the equator – getting non-noon sun

Cod-liver oil or vitamin A – which may hinder vitamin D 

Wearing extra clothes – by some religions or those fearing skin cancer

Benefits that people perceive of vitamin D

Problems that people have with vitamin D

Types of vitamin D – oil, spray, micro-tab, in food, …

Frequency of taking vitamin D: several times per day, daily, weekly, monthly…

Taken with big meal (found to double the serum level in blood 2x)

Cofactors taken/not taken: Calcium, Magnesium, etc.
